# Compatability

Wolves Of Other Furs supports the following mods:

- Oh The Biomes You'll Go!
- Wolves With Armor
- iChuns' Let Sleeping Dogs Lie

If you want your biome mod to support Wolves Of Other Furs, please ensure you add your biomes to the corresponding [Fabric Biome Convention Tag(s)](https://github.com/FabricMC/fabric/blob/1.19.4/fabric-convention-tags-v1/src/main/java/net/fabricmc/fabric/api/tag/convention/v1/ConventionalBiomeTags.java)

Wolves Of Other Furs has a few useful features exposed in the `WoofAPI` class - for more information, see the [API page.](/wolves-of-other-furs/api)
